This is an experimental project of mine. I wanted to try something new and combine my experience in 3D art with web design. I decided to go for a fictional interior design studio focusing on visualizations. To keep it simple I chose to create only hero section (and loading page, as it turned out).

> Bold design with high contrast

---

## Colors and Typography

As this was my personal project, I decided to keep it in colors I like: black and white. For typography I chose bold League Spartan font (very similar to timeless font Futura PT).

## 3D

3D models were kept to a very simple low-poly level, since everything would be either black or white - more detail would mostly only mean more distracting noise.

  <img src="/images/Torse_wireframe.png" alt="3D scene wireframe in Blender" />

## Shading

I used Freestyle rendering engine in Blender (often used to render lineart) and combined it with simple black&white shader rendered in EEVEE.

<figure>
  <img src="/images/Torse_shading.png" alt="Black and white shader in Blender" />
  <figcaption>Black and white shader in Blender</figcaption>
</figure>

---

## Technical details

The project was designed in Figma and developed with React and Motion animation library. All 3D work was done in Blender. Some image post-process was done using Photoshop.

## Final words

This was a fun small project. Hovewer, I wasn't too pleased with final result, as readability with pure black or white color didn't come out great. Also the final hero banner may be a little too noisy, a thing to keep in mind for the future
